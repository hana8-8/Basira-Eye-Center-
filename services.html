LASIK Eye Surgery

Permanent vision correction using laser technology. Most patients achieve 20/20 vision or better after the procedure.

  • Pre-surgery evaluation
  • Bladeless LASIK
  • Same-day procedure
  • Post-op follow-up care

Cataract Surgery

Removing the clouded natural lens and replacing it with a clear artificial lens — restoring sharp, clear vision.

  • Phacoemulsification technique
  • Premium IOL implants
  • Outpatient procedure
  • Rapid recovery
Book Now Learn more

Glaucoma Management

Early detection and long-term management of glaucoma to prevent vision loss caused by increased eye pressure.

  • Visual field testing
  • OCT nerve fiber analysis
  • Medical & laser treatment
  • Ongoing monitoring
Book Now Learn more

Diabetic Eye Care

Specialized screening and treatment for diabetic retinopathy — a leading cause of blindness in diabetic patients.

  • Retinal photography
  • Fluorescein angiography
  • Anti-VEGF injections
  • Laser photocoagulation
Book Now Learn more

Pediatric Eye Care

Gentle, child-friendly eye exams to detect vision problems early — because good vision is essential for learning.

  • Vision screening for children
  • Lazy eye (amblyopia) treatment
  • Squint (strabismus) correction
  • Kids' glasses fitting

Retina & Vitreous

Diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the retina, including macular degeneration and retinal detachment.

  • OCT imaging
  • Intravitreal injections
  • Retinal detachment repair
  • Vitrectomy surgery

Contact Lens Fitting

  • Lens care education
