State of the Art

Our Technology

Basira Eye Center invests in the most advanced diagnostic and surgical equipment available — because accurate diagnosis is the foundation of great care.

Gold Standard
Diagnostic Imaging

Zeiss Cirrus HD-OCT 6000

Optical Coherence Tomography produces cross-sectional images of the retina with microscopic resolution. Our Zeiss Cirrus captures 100,000 scans per second, detecting retinal diseases, glaucoma damage, and macular conditions years before they cause symptoms.

100,000 A-scans/second
5μm axial resolution
AI-powered analysis
Used for: Glaucoma Retina Diabetic Eye Care Macular Degeneration
Most Advanced
Laser Surgery

Alcon WaveLight EX500 Excimer Laser

The fastest and most precise laser platform for LASIK and PRK refractive surgery. At 500Hz repetition rate, it completes a full LASIK treatment in under 3 seconds per eye, with eye-tracking technology that follows every micro-movement for unparalleled accuracy.

500Hz repetition rate
1,050Hz eye tracker
<3 sec per eye
Used for: LASIK Surgery PRK Vision Correction
Precision Tool
Corneal Mapping

Pentacam AXL Wave

The Pentacam captures the full 3D geometry of the cornea and anterior segment in a single 2-second scan. It detects keratoconus at its earliest stage, maps the cornea before surgery, and measures the lens for IOL calculations with extraordinary precision.

138,000 elevation points
2 seconds scan time
Full 3D corneal map
Used for: Keratoconus Pre-LASIK Screening Cataract Planning Contact Lens
Ultra-Wide Field
Retinal Imaging

Optos Daytona Ultra-Widefield Camera

Traditional fundus cameras capture only 45° of the retina. The Optos Daytona captures 200° — 82% of the retinal surface — in a single image without pupil dilation. This allows us to detect peripheral retinal tears, detachments, and lesions that would otherwise be missed.

200° field of view
82% retinal surface
No dilation needed
Used for: Retinal Detachment Diabetic Retinopathy Peripheral Retina
Global Standard
Perimetry

Humphrey Field Analyzer 3

The worldwide gold standard for visual field testing. It maps the full extent of your peripheral and central vision, detecting the subtle losses caused by glaucoma, neurological conditions, and optic nerve disease — often before the patient notices any symptoms.

120° visual field coverage
SITA Faster AI algorithm
Fast 2-min test
Used for: Glaucoma Neurological Eye Disease Optic Nerve
Surgical Elite
Cataract Surgery

Alcon Centurion Vision System

The most advanced phacoemulsification system for cataract surgery. Its Active Fluidics technology maintains rock-solid intraocular pressure throughout the procedure, reducing surgical trauma and enabling faster recovery. The result: safer surgery, clearer vision, faster healing.

Active Fluidics pressure control
Ozil torsional ultrasound
Real-time IOP monitoring
Used for: Cataract Surgery IOL Implantation Complex Cataracts
