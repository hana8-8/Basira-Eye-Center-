Step by Step

How to Use Your Insurance

We make using insurance as easy as possible.

1

Book Your Appointment

When booking, tell us your insurance provider and policy number. We'll verify your coverage before your visit.

2

Bring Your Insurance Card

Bring your insurance card and a valid national ID to your appointment. Our desk staff will handle the rest.

3

We Handle Pre-Approval

For surgical procedures, we submit pre-approval requests to your insurer on your behalf — no calls needed from your side.

4

Pay Only Your Co-pay

For covered services, you only pay your policy's co-payment. We bill the rest directly to your insurance company.

Common Questions

Insurance FAQ

LASIK is typically considered an elective (non-essential) procedure and is not covered by most standard insurance plans. However, some premium plans do include partial refractive surgery coverage. We recommend calling your insurer directly or asking our desk team to verify before your consultation.

Yes, most surgical procedures require pre-approval from your insurance company. Our team handles this process entirely on your behalf — you just need to bring your insurance card and we take it from there. Pre-approval typically takes 1–3 business days.

A co-payment (or co-pay) is the fixed amount you pay out of pocket for a covered service. For example, if your plan has a 20% co-pay and your visit costs 500 EGP, you pay 100 EGP and your insurance pays the remaining 400 EGP. Your co-pay amount is printed on your insurance card.

Contact lens fitting and a basic supply of lenses is covered by some plans (like AXA and MetLife) but not all. It depends on your specific plan's "optical benefits". Our team can verify this before your appointment to avoid surprises at checkout.

We're always adding new insurance partners. Even if you don't see your provider listed, please call us or use the checker above — we may still be able to accept it. We also offer flexible self-pay payment plans for uninsured or underinsured patients.

Yes! We believe cost should never be a barrier to eye care. We offer flexible payment plans for surgical procedures, allowing you to spread payments over 3 to 12 months. Speak to our billing team during your visit or call us to learn more.
