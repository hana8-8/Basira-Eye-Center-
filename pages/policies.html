Privacy Policy

Basira Eye Center ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By accessing our website or scheduling an appointment, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not use our services.

Your medical records are protected under Egyptian healthcare privacy regulations and are never shared without your explicit written consent, except where required by law.

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as information we receive from your use of our website and services.

Personal Information

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Contact details (phone, email, address)
  • National ID number (for records)
  • Emergency contact information

Medical Information

  • Medical and eye health history
  • Prescriptions and test results
  • Treatment plans and records
  • Insurance and billing details

Website Usage Data

  • Browser type and IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Appointment booking behavior
  • Device type and operating system

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our medical services
  • Scheduling and managing your appointments
  • Sending appointment reminders and follow-up care instructions
  • Processing insurance claims and payments
  • Communicating important health updates and clinic news
  • Improving our website and online services
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using 256-bit SSL technology.

Access Controls

Only authorized medical staff can access your records, on a strict need-to-know basis.

Secure Servers

Your data is stored on secure, regularly backed-up servers with 24/7 monitoring.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and staff training to maintain the highest standards.

Your Rights

As a patient, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.

Right to Correction

You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Object

You can object to processing of your data for marketing purposes at any time.

Terms of Use

By using the Basira Eye Center website and services, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions.

Website Use

  • This website is for informational purposes and appointment booking only.
  • Content on this website is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
  • You must not misuse or attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems.
  • All content is the property of Basira Eye Center and may not be reproduced without permission.

Medical Disclaimer

The health information on this website is provided for general educational purposes only. It is not intended to be used as medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

Payment Policy

We aim to make payment as convenient as possible for our patients.

  • Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements are made.
  • We accept Visa, Mastercard, and cash payments.
  • Insurance claims are processed directly with your provider when applicable.
  • For complex procedures, a deposit may be required at the time of booking.
  • Itemized receipts are provided for all payments upon request.

For billing questions or to set up a payment plan, please contact our billing department at billing@basira.com.
